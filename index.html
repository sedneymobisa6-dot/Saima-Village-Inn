Our Story

A Taste of Home, Every Single Day

Saima Village Inn sits along Prison Road, next to Kisii Main Prison — a spot known to the whole town. We serve freshly prepared local Kenyan meals in a relaxed, friendly environment ideal for individuals, families, and work groups.

From morning mandazi and chai to evening nyama choma off the grill, every dish is made from fresh, locally sourced ingredients. No shortcuts. No freezers. Just honest food, made daily.
